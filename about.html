DEVCOM

Devcom is a technical community formed between the IEEE members of College of Engineering Chengannur. Starting with just four members, the community has now a strong student base. Devcom encourages peer to peer learning which enhances ones technological knowledge.Each members are mentored and his/her true potential is revealed.
